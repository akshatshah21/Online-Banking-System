Help

The home page gives an overview of the account and displays the balance present.
The Statement page gives you all the transactions that had been made through your account.
The make transactions page enables transferring money to your Beneficiaries.
The manage beneficiaries page allows you to make changes to your existing beneficiaries and also allows you to add new Beneficiaries.
The Logout button logs you out of the session and takes you back to the login page.
